Warm Welcome

My name is Joshua Graham and I am a graduating Virginia Tech student; where i am working to recieve my bachelors degree in techincal writing. I work hard to complete my assignments and participate in as many freelance projects as i can muster so that i can remain rounded. Most of my time is split between my school work and (excercising my creativity) writing screen-plays through individual writing gigs. I am passionate to complete projects as an individual or with a team. As an employee, i function best under time-constraints and high-stress enviorments. However, i know my greatest weakness is that if i am without an assignment i tend to stagnate. Fixing my stagnation usually leads me to start projects of differnt skill sets, such as: general carpentry, piano practice, painting, scrennplay-writing, coding, or general-contract work. I am most comfortable when i am busy, but i do relax when i hike, bike, or jog in the woods of southwest Virginia. Please go around the site and enjoy my work. Have a good day, and never forget sleep.
