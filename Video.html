Musicians on Campus

The Starving Artists are at it again with another look at arts and entertainment on the VT campus. We looked at 3 students who, despite thier other studies, take time to practice and pursue music. We want to encourage all students and professionals to flex that creative muscle and explore all avenues of preformance. We want to thank our subjects for taking part in our recordings and edits. All subjects have given consent for the video provided.
